Our Schools

Hekima schools

Hekima English medium Nursery primary and secondary schools are boarding schools found at Kabanga, Kasulu district in Kigoma region all neighbouring each other. The schools is located, 9 km from Kasulu town in Kabanga village. it has good and friendly environment for student learning.

The school has modern classes with electricity to support studies at night, it also fenced and with guards to improve security, it has enough sports fields and has dormitories with running water. Hekima schools offers excellent care for your child morally, spiritually, healthy and hospitality wise, the school has also good teaching staff with good learning techniques to make your children.

Our Awards

Hekima primary school was awarded the best school in Kigoma by NECTA national examinations commission in Tanzania in 2019, after claiming 1st place in Kigoma and 30th out of 9929 schools nationwide. While In 2020, school was 2nd in Kigoma and 41th out of 6641 schools nationwide
